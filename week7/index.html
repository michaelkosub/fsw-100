Quarter horse full-service breeding facility we are a family-owned business. A leading Quarter horse breeding farm, Kosub Breeding, breeds Quarter horses in a professional and safe environment. Kosub Breeding is dedicated to the physical, social and mental development of young horses, through a safe and healthy foaling process. Breeding your mare requires ongoing dedication to her needs and that of her foal. Kosub Breeding offers breeding, stud service, boarding, mare care and foaling, breaking, layups and sales preparation. The farm has numerous paddocks for turn out with adequate separation between mares and weanlings. You will not find a horse farm anywhere that strives to keep better facilities than Kosub Breeding.

Kosub Horse Breeding offers the following farm amenities:

  • 5 barns that are in mint condition
  • 20 large bedded stalls
  • Safe fencing throughout the farm
  • Small paddocks and large fields
